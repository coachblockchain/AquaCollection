I am pleased to introduce myself as a passionate and creative sculptor and graphic designer proficient in creating three-dimensional artworks and generating visual elements to communicate messages, using various artistic approaches and techniques depending on the discipline I am working in at the time.

My experience in the world of art and design has allowed me to combine two different disciplines, sculpture and graphic design, to create unique and expressive visual solutions. Here are some highlights of my experience and skills:

Sculpture:

I have worked in a variety of sculptural media, from modeling, ceramics, wood and stone carving to metal welding and the creation of large-scale outdoor murals. My sculptural work focuses on exploring forms, textures, and expressing emotional and abstract concepts through my creations. I have participated in both solo and group exhibitions, providing me with the opportunity to share my work with a wider audience.

Graphic Design:

I am proficient in using design tools such as the Adobe Creative Suite, enabling me to create engaging and effective visual materials. I have worked on branding, website design, logo creation, and promotional material design. I have a user-centered approach and focus on effective communication through design.

I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my perspective in designing projects as creative and innovative as NFT collections, creating unmistakable assets, both digital and physical, on the blockchain network, in collaboration with the COACHBLOCKCHAIN community.
